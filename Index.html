Ras Al Abyad Island Trip

Al-Ras Al-Abyad Island is one of the islands belonging to Al-Rais Governorate in Al-Madinah Al-Munawwarah Region. It is 20 km from the beach, and it takes 30 minutes to reach it by boat. It is considered one of the most beautiful beaches in the Red Sea and consists of pristine natural areas in the Kingdom, due to its nature which is suitable for swimming, diving and fishing. It also has stunning coral reefs. For lovers of diving and marine nature, there is a variety of scattered reefs, domes, grooves, and coral islands in the form of barriers. includes overnight stay.
